About Us

WHO WE ARE

We, a group of healthcare experts across the globe have teamed up to build sustainable, affordable, and standard of care, healthcare system in our community. The main purpose of AJ healthcare is to bring together healthcare experts to transform the future of healthcare across the entire continuum of care from primary care to tertiary care to after care. The combined strength is our team forms the core strength of an organization. Our diverse team is committed to achieving the mission and vision with utmost passion and attention to detail.
AJ team members have a proven track record of commissioning specialty clinics and hospitals across the globe as per Joint commissions international standards. Our team provides vast experience in different domains of healthcare like medical operations, quality, commissioning, improving business operations & P&L.
We believe that good healthcare management is rooted in effective processes, emotional intelligence, expert knowledge, and a strong understanding of and respect for local cultures.
